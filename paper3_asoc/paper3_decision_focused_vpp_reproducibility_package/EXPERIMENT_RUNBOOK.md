## Current Status

This package contains three article drafts, local data extracts, pilot baseline results, verified public reproducible experiments, target-journal submission packages, and a Chinese doctoral-dissertation integration working draft. Paper 1 now includes reviewer-facing GraphPatch robustness evidence, DLinear/NLinear-style modern sequence baselines, a sequence-anchored GraphPatch residual upgrade, and an EAAI-style anonymous manuscript, title/declarations file, highlights file, and checklist. Paper 2 now has verified references, masked-reconstruction transfer evidence, random-convolution representation strengthening, a trainable dilated-convolution ridge encoder check, a second public UCI Appliances Energy validation layer, an IEEE Access candidate package, and a sendable IEEE Access school-confirmation dispatch bundle. Paper 3 now has verified decision-focused/VPP references, inline citation markers, regenerated DOCX manuscripts, simulator appendix material, a public reproducibility supplement, an Applied Soft Computing target-journal package, and a sendable ASOC school-confirmation dispatch bundle. Cross-paper notation harmonization has been applied to Paper 1, Paper 2, and the dissertation drafts so price variables use `p_t^z`, resource-domain load variables use `ell_t^i`, and Paper 2 no longer conflicts with the VPP day index `d`. The dissertation draft integrates the three article manuscripts into a computer-science-framed seven-chapter thesis spine, with Chapter 2 expanded into a doctoral-level literature review and Chapters 3-5 expanded with fuller method, protocol, result-analysis, robustness, limitation, and chapter-summary prose for graph-temporal price forecasting, transferable load forecasting, and decision-focused VPP bidding.
